User Interface

These UI are created with Figma Tools

From left to right are: Landing Page, Login Page, Sign Up Page. These three pages are first view that will be seen in the application. The landing page are the first page that greets user. This page function as transition to showcase the logo before accessing the main apps. After the landing page is the login page. The user are asked to input their account if they already have their own account. If not the user will needs to create their account first.

From left to right are: Rent Menu, Home menu , Find menu. After the customer enter their account they will be greeted with the home menu. This Home menu are modeled affter Gojek Apps but with twist of simple ui for browsing through the menu. The next is Rent Menu in this menu the apps will prompt the user to open the camer and scan the barcode. After that there is the Find in this menu the user can find the location of Renting Helmet, they also shown the number of helmet that are being rented and ready for rent, just like the recharge apps.

For the turn back to home menu i put the arrow in the top left, the reason why i put that in there is because people tends to put turn back on the left and this apps also have feature slide to left to turn back.

From left to right are: History Menu and Settings Menu.In history menu the users can see their history transaction. For the completed transaction I give green at the status of transaction as seen at above but if the transaction are not done i give the yellow colored. For the setting menu if the user click on the widgets on setting it will give them the dropdown menu. Also for the logout button i give the color red but dimmer tu give the user warning of logout and encourage them to not logout because of the dimmer color.
